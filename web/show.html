The park has 2 stages for cultural and artistic activities and an animal circus with special performances of monkeys, bears, dogs, goats...

Star Stage

Also known as the island stage, is located directly in front of Au Lac Square. This performance spot is located in an oasis in the middle of Dam Sen Lake, with a width of 9m, a height of 6m, a depth of 8m. There are two entrances to the backstage, and the actors' dressing area. The sound system is fully equipped. Capacity 800 people. This place often takes place music and drama events with the participation of famous artists. This is considered an event venue suitable for all needs.


Star Stage of Hoa Binh Park

De Men Stage

Located in the children's area, near gate 2 of Hoa Binh Park. The hall is designed with ventilation, with a large fan. This system will limit the spread of respiratory diseases. At the same time, the light and sound system is equipped with just enough power for the children. Currently, this performance venue is always open on Saturdays and Sundays.



De Men stage for children at Hoa Binh Park

Animal circus

The animal circus is located in area B, in front of the entrance to the bird garden. Here every Saturday and Sunday there are always fun performances. The actors all came out from the wild world like monkeys, elephants, orangutans…. The children will be very excited to see the orangutan Samutra wearing a long dress cycling to school. Even the orangutan even came to take pictures with you. Or monkeys ride cyclos, go on stilts. And the dogs performed jumping around the circle. The purpose is to help children have a more friendly view of wild animals.
